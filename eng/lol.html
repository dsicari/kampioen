League of Legends

History

The title was announced on October 7, 2008. The Beta phase debuted on April 10, 2009, running until October 26 of that year. Then, counting on 40 champions and two modes of play: the classic "five-on-five" at Summoner's Rift and a "three-on-three" at Twisted Treeline, LOL was fully official.

The Basic

In the most classic mode of the game where each team has 5 members, players cross the map in order to reach the enemy base, but having to face numerous obstacles and sentinels. The game ends when one of the teams reaches and destroys the NEXUS (a kind of temple) of the enemy team.

Teams

The team is formed according to the type of game to be played, there are configurations 1x1, 3x3 and 5x5. Each member has the right to choose a champion from the more than 40 available, each avatar having a different specialty.

Game Time

There is no fixed time for the match, on average lasts 40 minutes, and can be extended for an unlimited time. There is no break and you can not change the champions during the match.
