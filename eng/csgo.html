Counter Strike Global Offensive

O Esporte

Counter Strike Global Offensive stunned the gaming industry when the unlikely MOD became the most played online PC action game in the world almost immediately after its release in August 1999.

History

Game developed by Valve Corporation and Hidden Path Entertainment, being a sequence of Counter-Strike: Source. It is the fourth main title of the franchise. It was released on August 21, 2012 for various platforms

O The Basic

What made CS the most famous was the gameplay of the Classic Competitive mode and one of the most played modes to date, where there is the terrorist side and the offensive, battling massively in order to achieve some goals such as: greater number of deaths, greater number of opponents killed, better base defense and so on.

Teams

The team is formed according to the availability of players, being a minimum of 1x1 and a maximum of 30x30.
