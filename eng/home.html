Exciting Sports

Get to know the saga of the sport that was born as a mal-finished version of English rugby and, in 151 years, has become one of the most exciting modalities on the planet. Certainly your friends ask you, "Why is this sport called 'football' if it's played with your hands?" To understand this, we need to go back in time, more specifically to the mid-19th century. At that time, many young people from families rich Americans went to live and study in Britain. And there they learned the two most popular sports: Association Football ("soccer") - played with feet and the round ball - and rugby football - a mode derived from the first, which included the use of hands as well. Upon returning to the United States, these students made the two sports scatter through the universities.
